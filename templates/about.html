Our history, Mission & Achievements

Founded in 1989Cantor College was established to specialise in Conputing and Design

Our Achievements

  • Strong links with world-leading researchers and employers
  • Graduates working successfully across the globe in diverse industries

  • Award-winning faculty with industry experience
  • 98% graduate employment rate within 6 months
