Student services

Student support services

Our dedicated student support team is here to help you throughout your time at Cantor College. From academic advice to personal support, we offer comprehensive services including tutoring, career guidance, financial aid information, and disability support.

Academic resources

Access a wealth of academic resources including our extensive library with over 50,000 books, online learning platforms, research databases, study spaces, and writing center support to enhance your learning experience.

Campus facilities

Enjoy modern facilities designed for your success including state-of-the-art computing labs, design studios with professional equipment, Student Hub with caf√©, fitness center, and 24/7 accessible study areas.

Clubs & societies

Get involved in our vibrant student community through clubs and societies including Game Development Society, Forensics Society, Programming Society, Design and Creative Arts Society, and various sports clubs.

Wellbeing & counselling

Your mental health and wellbeing are important to us. We offer free confidential counselling sessions, mental health support resources, stress management workshops, peer support programs, and crisis assistance.
